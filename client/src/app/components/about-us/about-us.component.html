About Us

StorageBox was created in 2023 with the primary aim of simplifying the process of listing and finding storage units. Previously, there were no platforms offering such services; individuals could only rely on contacting storage unit businesses directly. However, with StorageBox, we've introduced a centralized platform where storage unit owners can list their units, upload photos, and provide detailed information. Renters can now easily browse through these listings and contact the owners directly.
